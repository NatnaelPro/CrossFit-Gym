F. A. Q

How many calories do I need to burn a day to maintain my weight?

A person who wants to maintain their current weight needs to burn approximately 1,500 calories a day.

What are the best exercises for toning my body?

There is no one-size-fits-all answer to this question, as the best exercises for toning your body will vary depending on your individual fitness level and goals. However, some popular exercises that can help tone your body include weightlifting, cardio training, Pilates, and yoga.

How often should I go to the gym?

There is no one answer to this question. It depends on your goals, fitness level, and schedule.
